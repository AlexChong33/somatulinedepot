Somatuline Drug Delivery Device Patent Analysis Report

Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of the Somatuline Depot drug delivery device patent and identifies opportunities for developing a new, non-infringing drug delivery system with similar functionality.

Current Patent Status

The primary patent under consideration is US 11,850,410 B2, titled "AUTOMATICALLY RETRACTING SAFETY NEEDLE ASSEMBLY". This patent is central to the Somatuline Depot device's core technology.

Patent Number: US 11,850,410 B2

Title: AUTOMATICALLY RETRACTING SAFETY NEEDLE ASSEMBLY

Date of Patent (Issue Date): December 26, 2023

Assignee: DALI MEDICAL DEVICES LTD, Yavne (IL)

Application Number: 17/189,685

Filing Date: March 2, 2021

Focus: The patent details an automatically retracting safety needle assembly designed to receive a syringe. Key aspects include an internal housing, a moving sleeve biased by a biasing element, a syringe coupled to the moving sleeve, and an activation fork that controls the movement of the sleeve. The mechanism ensures automatic retraction of the syringe after injection, facilitating safe disposal and preventing needlestick injuries.

This patent provides a strong foundation for the device's needle safety features. Further analysis of related patents, prior art, and the competitive landscape is essential for identifying new innovation opportunities and potential freedom-to-operate issues.
